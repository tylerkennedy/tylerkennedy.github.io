F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I enable text message filtering

    1. Download our app from the app store
    2. Go to settings
    3. Scroll down and tap on Messages
    4. Scroll down and tap on Spam & Filtering
    5. Select Dont Text to allow our app to filter your messages

  • Do you have an android app?

    This app is only compatiable with iOS devices.

  • Can you read my text messages?

    No! Your privacy is important. This app won't read your messages and will never save or share any text message data from the app. The app only scans messages that you receive from unknown numbers. The app does not filter or read messages from saved contacts. It can only see the unknown sender's phone number and their message. It can not read messages that you send from your phone. Your information is never stored, shared, or sold.
